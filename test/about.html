Mayfair Parks Team

Mayfair Parks is led by Claronex Ltd. We are investors and new venture development specialists focused on housing, infrastructure, energy and water. Claronex is UK based with offices in South Asia, Europe and the US. We partner with leading design, technology and construction partners to deliver innovative and exciting projects.

Financing Options

We are working with leading financial partners to ensure cost effective competitive financing solutions. These will be instalment options, first time purchaser mortgages and second mortgage funding.

Property Management

Our community maintenance team will manage ongoing operation of the community. This will cover central management of security, water, power, lighting and waste collection. Mayfair Parks property management will also manage short term and long term home rentals.
