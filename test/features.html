Features

Location

Mayfair Parks is an exciting new project in the East of Lahore. Set across 25 acres and situated 20 minutes from the airport, Mayfair Parks brings together the benefits of clean air, clean water and it’s own integrated solar power generation and management system. This stylish, safe and secure gated community is designed around open park spaces with shops and leisure facilities. Mayfair Parks combines the benefits of quality, clean and comfortable living with the convenience being close to downtown Lahore.

• Landscaped communal park living in the East of Lahore

• 20 mins to Lahore International Airport

• 35 mins to Lahore City Centre

• 40 mins to Liberty Market
